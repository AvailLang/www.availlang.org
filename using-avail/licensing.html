Licensing

There are three types of licensing options for the use of Avail.

Individual licenses are available to individual hobbyists for personal use and personal or collaborative open-source projects.

General licenses are free and are for:

  • Accredited educational institutions for instructional purposes.
  • Independent contractors and businesses with three or fewer members and employees (combined).
  • Select nonprofit businesses and charities.

Enterprise licenses are required for those not qualified or approved for general use. These licenses require an annual subscription fee which is on a sliding scale according to the subscriber organization's annual revenue. Subscription fees are assessed annually.

At the discretion of the Avail Foundation, individuals who contribute significantly to the state of the art of Avail may be awarded royalty-free enterprise-level licenses for their individual use. These licenses are subject to annual review and revocation.

Obtaining a License

Registered users on AvailLang.org are automatically granted Individual licenses. To apply for a General or Enterprise license, please read and complete the following form: License Application
