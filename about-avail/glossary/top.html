Top

⊤
(Unicode character U+22A4: "down tack")

"Nothing". The abstract supertype of all types; the weakest and most general type. Top has a single instance, the which is accessible only to the Avail runtime.

Top serves as the "return type" for functions that do not produce a value. The ⊤ object is discarded implicitly when it is returned by an expression evaluation.
