condition

/avail/Avail/Foundation/Synchronization: condition
Source: /avail/Avail/Foundation/Synchronization
Categories: Synchronization, Data Abstractions, Types
Supertypes: object
A condition represents a situation that may be signaled or awaited from within a monitor. A condition maintains a parking queue of fiber s that are waiting for the condition to be signaled.
