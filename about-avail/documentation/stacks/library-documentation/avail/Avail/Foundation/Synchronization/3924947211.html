semaphore, []→⊤
Source: /avail/Avail/Foundation/Synchronization
Categories: Synchronization
Acquire a permit from the semaphore, then execute the specified function (as a critical section), then release the permit. If the semaphore is a binary semaphore, then multiple fiber s cannot concurrently execute the critical section; access is serialized. For a general counting semaphore, as may fibers as there are available permits are permitted to occupy the critical section concurrently.
