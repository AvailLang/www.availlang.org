Definitions:

future
Source: /avail/Avail/Foundation/Concurrency
Categories: Data Abstractions, Futures
Cancel evaluation of the specified future. Cancellation is best-effort only; the fiber responsible for evaluation of the future is not obligated to honor a termination request. Cancellation of a resolved future has no effect. Only the first cancellation attempt is permitted to have an effect. Subsequent cancellation attempts are silently ignored.
