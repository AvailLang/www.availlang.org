The Avail Team Homepage

All strictly private content pages will show the Members Only navigation menu. This navigation menu, its links, and the titles atop private pages will all appear in a distinct color. All of the content published for the use of Avail team members only will be linked from this page, the Members Only navigation menu, or both.

While we are figuring out how to make the public and private pages work together happily, the Members Only menu will only be visible on private pages. That means that if you click on a link to a public page, the menu will disappear. To get back to the private menu, use your browser's back button or type in the address of a private page manually. Keeping a private page bookmarked in your browser will be an easier way to get back.
